Spices

Cinnamon: A fragrant spice commonly used in both sweet and savory dishes, known for its warm and slightly sweet flavor.

Clove: A pungent spice with a strong, sweet aroma, often used in baking, cooking, and for its medicinal properties.

Turmeric: A vibrant yellow spice with a warm, bitter flavor, commonly used in curries, sauces, and as a natural food coloring.

Star Anise: A star-shaped spice with a strong, licorice-like flavor, used in both sweet and savory dishes, especially in Asian cuisine.
